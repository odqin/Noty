Privacy Policy for Noty Extension

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

Thank you for choosing Noty Extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Noty Extension collects and stores:

  • URLs and titles of websites you choose to save
  • Notes and content you manually create within the extension
  • Categories and tags you assign to saved items
  • User preferences such as dark/light mode settings

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and, if you're signed into Chrome, may sync across your devices through Chrome Sync.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Display your saved links and notes within the extension
  • Allow you to organize and categorize your saved content
  • Provide features like search, filtering, and favorites
  • Maintain your interface preferences

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
  • In your Chrome account if sync is enabled (subject to Google's privacy practices)

We do not:

  • Transmit your data to our servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Collect personally identifiable information

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Delete individual items or all data through the extension's interface
  • Disable Chrome Sync to prevent data synchronization across devices
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us
